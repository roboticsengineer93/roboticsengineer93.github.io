Hi, I'm Arjun S Kumar. I am an Engineer, specialized in Robotics and Machine Learning, whose working experience spans from top-ranking multi-national companies like Ernst & Young (EY) to mid-level and startup entrepreneurial ventures like Addverb Technologies, India and Ingeniarius, Lda., Portugal. I also worked as a robotics researcher at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, which is one of the top universities in the world today.

I'm currently working as a Senior Engineer in Machine Learning at a Semiconductor startup firm Ignitarium Technologies in India. My journey toward professional and research excellence began with a Master's degree in Robotics & Automation from Amrita School of Engineering, Amritapuri Campus, India.

Today, my journey has led me to my passion: to work on cutting edge applications of computer vision and deep learning in robotics (mobile robots and autonomous vehicles in particular). I'm highly interested in the fields of Computer vision, Deep learning, Path planning, Robot autonomy and Product development.

My dream (nay, my life's purpose!) is to enhance human-robot interaction through my research that augments the quality of human life.
